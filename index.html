About

My name is Greg Lindquist; a full stack developer focusing on c#/.net with over a decade of experience in the industry. In that time I've done work for big brands spanning from Gatorade to Tropicana, Lionel Trains to the Indianapolis Motor Speedway and more.

I love building new experiences in a collaborative environment. My primary personality trait is to be an explorer, and I'm constantly looking at what's new in .net or any frontend framework. I also love to share my newly found knowledge with others and see how everyone takes the information. Sometimes, the most creative solutions come from with a seed of an idea and germinate with the rest of the team. If asked what my decade + of experience has taught me, it's that while it's possible to build something with a team of one, it's a magical experience to build with a team of telented people.
